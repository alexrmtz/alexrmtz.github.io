Interview Summary:
You recognize you need technology to meet your mission. So how do you create a grant proposal to secure the funding you require not only to do your work but also with the appropriate technology to support it? It's definitely a challenge, but there are tactics and strategies you can employ to express your technology needs. 

This webinar will focus on integrating your technology requests into grant proposals to fund the technology demands of your organization. Cynthia Adams, CEO of GrantStation, has over 35 years of grantseeking experience, and will address the challenges and give you some practical steps for your next grant.

Learning Outcomes:

    * How do you integrate tech needs into capacity building requests? 
    * When is it appropriate to build a grant request solely focused on technology?
    * How do you incorporate tech needs into general operating grant requests?
