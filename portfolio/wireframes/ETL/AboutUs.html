Educating Tomorrow’s Lawyers is an initiative of the Institute for the Advancement of the American Legal System at the University of Denver. The purpose of Educating Tomorrow’s Lawyers is to build upon the Carnegie  Foundation “Educating Lawyers” model to encourage legal education dedicated to the highest standards of professionalism. The project will create a network of law schools committed to working and learning together to improve legal education.  The project will sponsor the exchange of ideas and gatherings of educators, researchers, and practitioners; document and publicize exemplary innovations through a comprehensive website; and ultimately develop resources for improving and assessing the quality of law teaching and the quality of law graduates.
 
Sponsored by the Institute for the Advancement of the American Legal System (IAALS)
