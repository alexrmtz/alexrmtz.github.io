We believe it is imperative that we address changes in the external environment. In particular, we must:
• Adjust to recent changes in the legal profession including changes in the organization and economics of legal practice, in the relationship between lawyers and clients, and in the increasing need for access to justice;
• React to changes in educational theory and practice that have swept the nation in the last several years;
• Adapt to a changing world by considering the impacts on law and legal practice of phenomena such as globalization; increasing diversity; technological change; and the growing inter-connectedness of peoples and places.
