Teaching Criminal Justice Ethics Using Collorative Learning
Description:
Date Added:  Jan 17 2010
Author:  Jack Torres
University: University of New Mexico
This material examines ethical issues questions of right and moral wrong -- confronting the criminal justice system, its processes, and personnel (e.g., police officers, attorneys and judges, correctional personnel).
Learning Outcomes:
• Communicate effectively using listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills.
• Solve problems using critical and creative thinking and scientific reasoning.
• Formulate strategies to locate, evaluate, and apply information.
Creative Commons:
 
This module includes:
• Three in-class activities
• Handouts for each activity
• Assessment questions
