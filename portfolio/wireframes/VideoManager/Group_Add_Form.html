VideoManager Groups

DU instructors and staff members can create a VideoManager group to allow students to submit their videos for review. 

Group Name

Instructions

1.  Create a group name below.

2.  Send students the group invite email.

3.  Have students log into VideoManager with the invite email.

4.  Students will see the group they were invited to once they login.

5.  Students will upload their video.

6.  The group manager will be notified once the student video is ready to be seen.

7.  The group manager can add video comments for each student video submitted.

8   The group manager can share the video along with the comments to the student.
